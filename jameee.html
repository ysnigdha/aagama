Description

Event Outline:

The event will consist of two rounds. The strength of the team must be 2 members.
Round 1(Preliminary round):
In this round, team needs to pitch on the topic which is selected by them in just one minute. Teams who reached the benchmark of judges will be moved to next round.
Round 2(Finals):
In this round, teams need to pick one chit from group of chits. Team will be given 10 minutes to discuss about the topic about the topic. Team needs to pitch about the topic in just one minute. Team which performs good and meets the requirements are winners.

Rules

  • Any student within the age group of 14- 25 years can participate and each team should consist of not more than three members.
  • Use of mobile phones or any other electronic or communication device is prohibited during the event.
  • Participants should carry their college or school ID cards.
  • Organizers reserve the right to disqualify teams on the grounds of misbehavior and use of unfair means.
  • Teams exceeding the time limit are liable for disqualification.
