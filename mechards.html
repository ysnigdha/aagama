It's time to awaken the mechanical engineer inside you. Here is a platform to showcase your technical as well as management skills. This event is to test your teamwork reflexes, technical knowledge and awareness.

This event consists of 3 rounds- 1) Unitech 2) Tom and Hurry 3) Fast and Curious
Structure:
Round 1- Unitech :
Each team will be provided with a box which contains tokens with alphabet or letter or number on it. Any parameter or quantity is announced for which the team has to guess the unit.
Round 2- Tom and Hurry:
One of the team members will be given a card which has a mechanical component's name or word which he has to enact and other person has to guess the word within the time limit and arrange the letters of the word.
Round 3- Fast and Curious: A person is blindfolded and given shuffled letters. He/she has to arrange it as the second person directs to form a mechanical term.
Rules :
1) A team shall consist of 2 members
2) The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile and other electronic devices.
3) Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.
4) Any kind of indiscipline activity the team will be disqualified.
