Description

A Word Challenge
A spell bee is a competition in which contestants are asked to spell a broad selection of words, usually with a varying degree of difficulty.

Rules

  • Participants have to register in advance for all events
  • Spot registrations will not be entertained
  • Single participant
  • 50 seconds of time will be given to each participant
