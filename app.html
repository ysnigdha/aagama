Description

In this event the participants must provide a solution to the real time problems. Prototype should be given for the application that they want to develop. Their idea can be presented using Microsoft power-point (2007, 2010).

  • Coding knowledge is not required.

Rules

  • The prototype should not contain any content that is inappropriate.
  • The content must not be copied from any websites and any false information leads to team elimination.
  • The idea and prototype should not be modified once it is presented.
  • The participants have to submit their ideas in the abstract form before 14th March,2017 to aagama2k17cse@cvsr.ac.in
  • Come along with your abstract (hard and soft copy).
  • Presentation is to be carried in pendrives only.
  • There can be 1 or 2 members per team.
Registration fee: Rs.50/- per head.
Best idea presented will be awarded.
