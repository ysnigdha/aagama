Description

In this Event , all the participants have to prepare any civil structure of their own wish with in a stipulated time period . It is an ultimate opportunity for everyone to exhibit their architectural skills.

Rules

  • College ID is mandatory.
  • Material will be provided.
  • Each team comprises maximum of 3 members.
  • Models must be prepared within the given time limit.
  • Other materials will not be provided.
  • Best models among all will be awarded.

Structure

Straws and use and thrown glass material will be given to make their model.
