Description

Let's learn technical words adding some more fun.

Rules

  • Each team consists of 3 members
  • The game will have three levels.
  • The participants are not allowed to use any electronic gadgets.

Structure Of The Quiz

Level 1 : They need to pickup one slip and enact the word within the time limit.
Level 2 : In this level they can enact how many words they can.
Level 3 : In this level in given time they should enact one word and should tell application on that particular word.
Based on this teams are selected and will be awarded the prize money.
