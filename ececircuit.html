Circuit Trix

Event Description

An event which tests the very basics of electronics.

Rules

� A team shall consists of max two persons.
� The participants shall not use any mobile or other electronic instruments.
� The questions shall be in the form of puzzles, analyzing circuits and building circuits.
� There is time limit for every round.
� Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.
