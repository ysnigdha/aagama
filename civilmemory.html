Description

In this event ,all the technical and general things are displayed one by one infront of participants and they have to list out the same as per their memory thereafter .

Rules

  • 5 minutes to observe the displayed things.
  • 5 minutes to list out the displayed things.
  • No audience.
  • Individual performance.
  • Malpractice is strictly prohibited.

Structure

The things will be displayed and participants have to list out the maximum number of things displayed.
