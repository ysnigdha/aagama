Description

Do you believe that fun can be found from junk? Funk From Junk gives an opportunity to CRAZ'C student to showcase their technical wackiness combined with the old fashioned tricks to strengthen their understanding with their teammates..!!!

Rules

  • A team shall consist of max two persons or min one person.
  • The decision of the quiz master will be the final and will not be subjected to any changes.
  • The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments
  • The questions will be in the form of circuit diagram pictures.
  • Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.
