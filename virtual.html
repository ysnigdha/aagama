Description

This is a platform where you can experience and improve your knowledge and skills. This event consists of 3 rounds

  • Aptitude Test
  • Group Discussion
  • HR Round

Rules

  • A person should individually participate.
  • The Participant will not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.
  • The Questions will be in the form of multiple choice questions.

Structure

  • Round 1 (Aptitude test)-A total of 30 aptitude questions are given. The top 40% members will be qualified to the next round.
  • Round 2 (Group Discussion)-Conducted by the HR. The selected candidates will be qualified for next round.
  • Round 3(HR round)-personal interview with HR.
