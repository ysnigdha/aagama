Laser Trap

Event Description

Laser Trap is an interactive game
in which players race against a maze
of lasers to reach the end point
as quickly as possible.

Rules

1.ID card must be carried.
2.Single player game.
3.Proper discipline should be maintained.
4.Player should wear laser safety glasses if provided.

CAUTION 1. Extreme care should be taken while playing as lasers are harmful.
2. Player should not maintain direct eye contact with lasers.
* Registration fee is 100/- per person
