EVENT DESCRIPTION:
A picture speaks a thousand words indeed! Visual appeal + Good Presentation = Winning Combination!

Rules and Regulations:

1. Each team comprises maximum of 2 members.
2. One hard copy should be submitted on the day of presentation.
3. The abstracts should be relevant to the topics specified.
4. College ID cards are mandatory.
5. Cross college teams are also allowed with valid ID-proof.
6. The decision of the organizers is final and binding.
7. The final poster is to be in the form of a flexi, with standard dimensions of 2.5' by 2.5'. (Charts are also accepted but higher priority will be given for flexi).
8. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited; points will be awarded based on the extent of innovativeness, presentation and skill.
9. If the work is a review, then it will be allowed to be presented only on acknowledgment of the relevant authors.
10. Spot registrations are also accepted.
Other topics are acceptable, provided they are related to the branch.
