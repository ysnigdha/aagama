Event consists of total 3 rounds.
Round 1:
1. Participants should form a group of minimum two or maximum three students.
2. Participants must carry a valid identity card of their respective institutions.
3. Questions will be asked from the topics of electrical Engineering.
Round 2:
1. After Prelims, only 10 top scoring teams will be selected for next rounds.
2. After Rapid Fire round only 6 teams will be selected for Final round.
Round 3:
1. After the final round 2 highest scoring teams will be declared as winners.

Rules and regulations:

1. Any student within the age group of 14- 25 years can participate and each team should consist of not more than three members.
2. Use of mobile phones or any other electronic or communication device is prohibited during the event.
3. Participants should carry their college or school ID cards.
4. Organizers reserve the right to disqualify teams on the grounds of misbehavior and use of unfair means. Their decision is considered final and not subjected to dispute.
5. Teams exceeding the time limit are liable for disqualification.
